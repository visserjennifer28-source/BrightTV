# BrightTV
Viewership analytics project focused on boosting user engagement and subscription growth for BrightTV.

BrightTV Viewership Analytics Case Study
* Overview
This project analyzes user behavior and viewership patterns for BrightTV, a digital streaming platform.
The objective is to generate data-driven insights that support the Customer Value Management (CVM) team in growing the platform’s subscription base.

The analysis focuses on identifying user trends, understanding consumption drivers, and recommending strategies to increase engagement and acquire new users.

# * Objectives
Analyze user demographics and behavior
Identify viewership and consumption trends
Determine key factors influencing engagement
Recommend content strategies for low-consumption periods
Propose initiatives to grow BrightTV’s user base

# * Key Insights
Peak Usage occurs during evenings (18:00–22:00) and weekends
Midweek (Tue–Thu) shows significantly lower engagement
Majority of users are young adults (18–34)
Series content drives longer sessions and higher engagement
A small group of heavy users contributes most of total consumption
# * Problem Identified
انخفاض engagement during midweek periods
Uneven distribution of user activity
Risk of churn among low-activity users
# * Recommendations
# *Content Strategy
Release binge-worthy series midweek
Introduce short-form content (20–30 mins)
Invest in local South African content
Add live events (sports, reality shows)
# *Engagement Strategy
Personalized recommendations
“Continue Watching” reminders
Push notifications for new releases
Midweek promotions and campaigns
# *Growth Strategy
Referral programs (“Invite a friend”)
Student discounts and targeted offers
Partnerships with telecom providers
Free trials and freemium content
# *Expected Impact
Increased user engagement
Improved retention rates
Growth in subscription base
Higher overall watch time
